Jin-Chuan Shi

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen and Prof. Hao Chen. My research focuses on hand–object interaction reconstruction and retargeting, with an emphasis on dexterous hand manipulation and control.

I received my B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from Beihang University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Miao Wang and Prof. Shimin Hu.
